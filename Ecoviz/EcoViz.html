About Our Mission

EcoViz Space is dedicated to bringing awareness to the critical issue of global warming through innovative data visualization. Our app combines real-world climate data with an engaging space theme to help users understand the long-term impacts of climate change on our planet and beyond.

App Features

Interactive Globe

Explore temperature changes across the Earth with our 3D globe visualization.

Time Travel

View past climate data and future projections to see the evolution of global warming.

Cosmic Perspective

Understand Earth's climate in the context of our solar system and beyond.

Data-Driven Insights

Access comprehensive climate data from reliable scientific sources.
